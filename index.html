About School

TM International School is an eminent educational institution established under magnificent aegis of Gyan Mangal Education and Charitable Sanstha, Bharatpur. It aims at helping the children from far flung rural areas to have an easy access to Modern Education System and Explore, Evoke their personality traits so as to overcome the problems of life such as indiscipline, impertinence, frustration and any perverted habit through regular toiling and endeavour by being noble, contained and civilized citizen of our great INDIA. Taking a clue from intuition spurred by a ‘Galaxy of Educationist’ it will be our endeavour to cultivate sensibility, sensitivity and creativity of the students in addition to their intellectual development. TMIS is situated in a perfect Indian setting yet delivers an International Education. The building is modern and spacious and offers the students the nearest comfort of home within the city at Rajasthan’s Eastern Gate, The Brij Bhoomi also known as the ‘Lohagarh’, i.e. Bharatpur. The education system besides providing an all round education intellectually, socially, emotionally and physically, also offers guidance in critical thinking, social openness and in purposeful research. Students are given the opportunities to develop their own talents and the same time to be aware of National and Global issues, while contributing to the healthy development of the needs of their own school campus and community.

Aajivanam Gyan Vardhanam

The School motto as expressed in Sanskrit ‘AAJIVANAM GYAN VARDHANAM’ reflects that life and learning are one. The latent translation is ‘Life is for learning’ or ‘Learning for life.’ ‘VITA EADEM STUDIA,’ the three expressions are open to very rich interpretation and the choice of two ancient languages is the representation of the fusion of cultural excellence found at TM International School.
